Origin Story

You know what they say, the best way to a person's heart is through their stomach.

The owners were inspired by the beautiful culture of their country, Thailand, that they wanted to share it with others through the language of food.

As soon as they immigrated to Las Vegas, NV, they decided to open their own restaurant to fulfill their dreams of sharing their culture.
